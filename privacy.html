Privacy Policy for RozgarAI

Last Updated: November 8, 2025

RozgarAI ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). Please read this policy carefully. By using the App, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our services. The types of information we collect depend on how you use our App.

1.1. Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: When you register, we collect personal identifiers, including your Name and Email Address.
  • Profile Information: To personalize our services, you may voluntarily add information to your profile, such as your Education, Work Experience, Location, desired Job Role, and Skills.
  • User-Generated Content: We collect and store the content you create within the App's features, including:
    • Your Chat History with the AI.
    • Your practice answers for the HR and Technical Self-Assessment features.
    • Your saved STAR Method stories.
    • Your Company Research notes.
    • Your Checklist tasks and associated Reminder Times.
    • Your list of Saved (Bookmarked) Questions and Links.

1.2. Information Collected Automatically

  • Audio Data (Voice Recordings): We collect audio recordings *only* when you actively use the voice input (microphone) feature. This data is sent to our backend for transcription and is **deleted immediately after processing**. It is not stored.
  • Usage and Device Data: We use services like Firebase Analytics to collect information about your device (e.g., model, OS version) and how you interact with our App (e.g., features used, session duration). This data is often aggregated and anonymized.
  • Crash Reports: We use Firebase Crashlytics to automatically collect reports on app crashes, including device state and stack traces, to help us diagnose and fix bugs.

2. How We Use Your Information (Purpose of Collection)

We use your information for the following purposes, which are necessary for app functionality, personalization, and analytics:

  • To Provide and Operate the App: To create your account, authenticate you, store your notes and saved items, and run the core features.
  • To Personalize Your Experience: To use your profile (skills, job role) to generate personalized AI responses, provide relevant job recommendations, and create custom study guides.
  • To Power AI Features: To send your chat messages and self-assessment answers to our AI partners to generate responses and feedback.
  • To Enable App Features: To transcribe your voice, find job listings from APIs, and schedule the task reminders you set.
  • To Communicate With You: To send notifications for chat replies or deliver your scheduled alarms and reminders.
  • To Improve and Secure Our App: To analyze usage trends, fix bugs, and prevent fraudulent activity.

3. How and Why We Share Your Information (Third-Party Data Sharing)

We do not sell your personal data. We only share information with the following trusted third-party service providers as necessary to operate our App:

Service Provider Data Shared Purpose
Backend Host (e.g., Render) Profile Data, User Content, Queries To host our core application logic that connects the app to all other services.
Firebase (Google) Email, Name, Profile Data, All User-Generated Content, Usage Data For user authentication, secure database storage (Firestore), crash reporting, and analytics.
AI Providers (e.g., OpenAI, Google Gemini) Chat messages, Chat history (for context), Self-Assessment answers To generate all AI-powered chat responses and feedback. We do not send your name or email.
Speech-to-Text (e.g., Google Cloud) Audio Recordings (temporary) To transcribe your voice input into text. Audio is deleted immediately after processing.
Job Search APIs (e.g., Jsearch) Non-identifying search queries (e.g., "Software Engineer in New York") To fetch relevant, real-time job listings.

We may also disclose your information if required by law, such as in response to a subpoena or legal process, or to protect our rights and the safety of our users.

4. App Permissions Explained

Our App requests the following permissions to enable specific features:

  • Internet (android.permission.INTERNET): Required for all network activity, including logging in, communicating with our backend, fetching AI responses, and loading job data.
  • Microphone (android.permission.RECORD_AUDIO): Required *only* if you use the voice input (microphone) feature. This allows the App to capture your speech for transcription.
  • Notifications (android.permission.POST_NOTIFICATIONS): (On Android 13+) Allows the App to send you notifications for chat replies (when the app is in the background) and for task reminders from your checklist.
  • Set Exact Alarms (android.permission.SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM): (On Android 12+) This permission is required *only* if you use the "Set Reminder" feature in the checklist. It allows the app to schedule and trigger your task reminders reliably.

5. Data Security

We are committed to protecting your data. We use industry-standard measures, including:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS (TLS/SSL).
  • Encryption at Rest: Your data (such as profile information, chat history, and notes) is stored in our database (Firebase Firestore) in an encrypted state.

While we take reasonable steps to secure your information, no electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention and Deletion Rights

Retention: We retain your Personal Data for as long as your account is active or as necessary to provide you with our services.

Deletion: You have the right to request the deletion of your account and all associated personal data. You can do this by:

  1. Navigating to the "Profile" or "Settings" section within the App and selecting the "Delete Account" option.
  2. Contacting us directly at the email address provided below to request deletion.

Upon receiving a valid request, we will permanently delete your personal data from our active systems within a reasonable timeframe, subject to any legal retention requirements.
